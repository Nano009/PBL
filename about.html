About us

Our Mission

At Venture Box, we focus on the mission of alleviating the struggles and predicaments that start-ups and young enterprises face. We work relentlessly, with passion, to turn nations into business powerhouses, grow economies, and change the world.

Our Vision

We dream of a world which possesses a future where every outstanding idea can be shaped, cultivated, and thrive together at Venture Box. We firmly believe that our strong dedication will lead us to such reality.

How Venture Box Started...

Venture Box began when Alan, the then entry-venture-capitalist, had an idea of establishing an agency to provide services and knowledge that would help prospective start-up CEOs or like-minded venture capitalists get their best out of the industry. He began a personal blog that revolved around finance, investing and cultivating business ideas.

After curating a group of audience, he gathered his friends, Nano, James, and Sat, and developed Create.co, and provided paid services related to creativity.

With the funds from Create.co and help from other VCs, the group founded Venture Box, and raised an international unicorn, various global corporations and helped many local start-ups thrive.
